Welcome to Farmtronics! Enter MiniScript
code at the prompt. For language help,
see: `http://miniscript.org`

When typing a command, the computer will
sometimes make suggestions in faint text
after the cursor.  Just press the `Tab`
key to accept the suggested text.

To break out of any program, press
Control-C. To clear the screen, enter
`clear`

For more help, enter `help "topics"`
Remember to use quotation marks!
